Current-truth mockups for the Finnish water-utility SaaS

These mockups are grounded in the current repo truth and the generated spec file: 5 visible setup steps in Yhteenveto, a compact support rail, the shared year-card object across step 2 and step 3, a baseline checkpoint in step 4, a narrow handoff in step 5, a scenario-led Ennuste board, and a document-oriented Raportit surface.

Source spec: finnish-water-utility-saas-frontend-spec.md Repo truth: apps/web/src/v2/** Palette grounded in apps/web/src/v2/v2.css Labels grounded in apps/web/src/i18n/locales/fi.json
